Welcome to Jira Board Enhancer!

Here’s how to get started and manage your settings.

How It Works

Automatic Detection

When you visit a Jira site that isn't enabled yet, the extension icon will show a question mark badge.

Just click the icon, and Chrome will ask for your permission to activate the extension for that site.

Board Requirements and Configuration

For the plugin to work correctly, your Jira board must meet two conditions:

1. Use Swimlanes

The feature to collapse the first column depends on the board being organized by Swimlanes (e.g., by "Assignees", "Epics", or "Queries"). If your board does not use swimlanes, this feature will not be activated.

2. Display the "Sub-Tasks" Field

For sub-tasks to appear on the cards, you must add the "Sub-Tasks" field to the card view in your board's configuration.

You can do this from your board: Board settings → Card layout and add "Sub-Tasks" to the visible fields section.

Manage Jira Instances

You can also manually add your Jira site URLs here. Enter the base URL (e.g., https://your-company.atlassian.net).
